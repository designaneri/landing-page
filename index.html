DigiSign Signing Flow

Document Upload

  • A user uploads the document(s).
  • Selects verification criteria e.g. biometrics, hardware key, OTP authentication, live Video/Image proof, Aadhar KYC etc.
  • Selects signers and observer(s)

Signatures

  • Signers authenticate themselves and sign based on the criteria set by the document owner

Blockchain Stamping

  • Document is timestamped in the Blockchain to make it tamper-proof and secure.
